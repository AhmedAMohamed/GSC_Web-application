Google Student Club “GSC”

is the 1st Google student activity in the Egyptian universities , GSC was launched in fall 2013 at Faculty of Engineering , Ain-shams university aiming to be a campus contact to Google teams and delivering Google's experience and values to our community.

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Vision
Providing the students with all the needed information about Google's competitions concerning how and when to apply, and helping them to be qualified to participate in it, we also seek the availability of information about internships at Google to graduates and undergraduates, and even how to create a company similar to it.
 
Mission
Offering (workshops/ open sessions/ events) instructed by Googlers or students, in both technical and non technical fields, for all students.
